ABOUT

Dominion City Brewing Co. is an Ottawa Valley brewery-in-the-making.

Anchored in the traditions of Canada’s brewing history, Dominion City Brewing Co. brews quality, distinctive, boundary-pushing beer that is closely tied to the people and the land that make it possible.

Our products celebrate the best of the past, including rare and lost beer styles, as well as embracing new flavours and experimental ingredients.

We brew our beer using heirloom organic specialty grains grown and milled right here in the Ottawa Valley. It’s our way of producing a unique product that celebrates the best of our region.

We’ve got a vision and a plan for a business built on great beer. We're building a company that supports our local community while celebrating its past. We’re going to do it by emphasizing a connection with the people and land that make our beer possible.

If you would like to know more or want to be involved, contact us.
